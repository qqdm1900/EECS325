24.9 Configuring the Stepper

To configure the Stepper tool, raise the Preferences dialog, by choosing LispWorks > Preferences... or clicking . Then select Stepper in the list on the left side of the Preferences dialog.

The Stepper Preferences have three tabs:

  • The General tab controls display of the Stepper toolbar, as described in Toolbar configurations.
  • The Operators tab contains options controlling the behavior when the stepper sees functions or macros in the source.
  • The Backtrace tab controls the amount of information shown automatically in the Backtrace area.
