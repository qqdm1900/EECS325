25 The CLOS Package

This chapter describes the LispWorks extensions to CLOS, the Common Lisp Object System.

The LispWorks Meta Object Protocol mostly conforms to chapters 5 & 6 of AMOP. Manual pages for symbols with different functionality from AMOP are in this chapter, and the differences are discussed in The Metaobject Protocol.
