A.4.2 Multiple Goals

To request the solution of multiple goals, use: (and < goal1 > ... < goalN >)

For example:

|==> (and (member ?x (2 3)) (append (?x) (foo) ?y))
|
|X = 2
|Y = (2 FOO)
|
|OK.
|
|==>
